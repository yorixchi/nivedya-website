COURSES ON OFFER

Two licences worth of training, one school.

Whether you're driving your own car for the first time or training for a commercial licence to earn a living, the syllabus is built around the test — and around real Trivandrum traffic.

LMV

In-Car Driving

Hands-on training in a private car — clutch control, city traffic, parking, and test-route practice for your LMV licence, one-on-one with a trainer.

Structured training for a commercial driving licence — built for gear, load, and long-vehicle handling, with the discipline commercial permits require.
